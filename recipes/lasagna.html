Recipe for Lasagna

  1. Gather the ingredients:
    • 9 lasagna noodles
    • 1 pound (450 grams) ground beef or Italian sausage
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
    • 1 can (14 ounces or 400 grams) crushed tomatoes
    • 1 can (6 ounces or 170 grams) tomato paste
    • 2 teaspoons dried basil
    • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    • 2 cups (500 grams) ricotta cheese
    • 2 cups (200 grams) shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 1/2 cup (50 grams) grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped (optional)
  2. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine. Simmer the sauce for about 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the noodles and set them aside.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese and half of the shredded mozzarella cheese. Add some salt and pepper to taste. Stir in the chopped fresh basil leaves if desired.
  7. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  8. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, slightly overlapping them. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  9. Add another layer of meat sauce, followed by another layer of noodles and ricotta cheese. Repeat the process until all the ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  10. Sprinkle the remaining shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese over the top of the lasagna.
  11. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the lasagna is heated through.
  12. Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy!
