Recipe for Pizza

  1. Gather the ingredients
    • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 cup warm water
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, instant yeast, sugar, and salt.
  3. Gradually add the warm water and olive oil to the dry ingredients. Mix with a spoon or your hands until a dough forms.
  4. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead it for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover it with a clean kitchen towel. Allow it to rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours or until it doubles in size.
  6. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) or the temperature recommended by your pizza stone or baking sheet instructions.
  7. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release any air bubbles. Transfer it to a floured surface and divide it into two equal portions if making two pizzas.
  8. Roll out the dough into a round shape or your desired shape and thickness. If needed, use a rolling pin to help achieve an even thickness.
  9. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone. If using a baking sheet, you may want to sprinkle some cornmeal or flour on the sheet to prevent sticking.
  10. Add your desired pizza toppings, such as tomato sauce, shredded cheese, vegetables, meats, and herbs. Be creative with your choices!
  11. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  12. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Slice it into pieces and serve hot.

Enjoy your homemade pizza!
